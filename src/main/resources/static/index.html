About Us

DEPRA was born from a dream stitched with stubborn hope. In the year 2025, Priya stood at the edge of uncertainty, holding vision in one hand and courage in the other. There were late nights, silent doubts, and days where the world felt too heavy — but she kept walking. One thread at a time, one brave decision at a time, she opened this store like a lamp lit in the dark.

This brand is not just about fashion. It is about the quiet thunder of a woman who refuses to disappear. It is about coffee-colored resilience, pink-peach softness, and the ancient dignity of tradition meeting a modern life.
